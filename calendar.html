The EDM Calendar

The EDM calendar is a resource for EDM BFA and Digital Design BA students that contains month-by-month deadlines for competitions, awards, internships and other recurring opportunities.

To follow up on an opportunity, contact the EDM club or Prof. Muyuan He. For Internships, be sure you have downloaded the Internship FAQ before contacting Prof. Muyuan He.


To post an event, competition or other opportunity contact Prof. Weintraub or Prof. Muyuan He, and put the opportunity information, with contacts, either in the body of an email or in an attachment. All opportunities will be reviewed by faculty before posting.
