Allen Guzman

Athenic

Process Book

Abstract: For my thesis project, I’ve created branding for the women-centered weightlifting gym, Athenic, aimed to inform, inspire, and initiate change for women in weight rooms. The name Athenic comes from the mythical Greek goddess Athena and the word athletic. This name is meant to represent the empowerment and strength of women. This project aims to create a more comfortable weightlifting experience for women by offering an empowering, judgment-free environment.

My main design element is a website containing information on weightlifting and women’s health, which also promotes the gym’s programs and benefits. Three infographics, using photographs and text, showcase positive research and the benefits of weightlifting for women’s health, as well as the negative effects stemming from harassment in a gym environment. I have created an Instagram page to promote women empowerment related to fitness, which is also used as a platform to post photography of female weightlifters working out. Flyers have been designed to both promote Athenic’s programs and benefits and to inform the audience of the negative impact of harassment in gyms. Interior-space mockups further showcase the branding graphics—including an owl mascot, type treatments, and photography—to showcase the Athenic gym. Brand gear also includes a care box that comes with a premium subscription with a welcoming card, a wristband, a water bottle, t-shirts, and stickers with the brand’s logo.

Project Elements

Gym

This is a layout of Athenic’s physical gym space with workout equipment arranged in a circle. At the center of the gym is an image of the mascot with the brand’s slogan.

Workout Equipment

Athenic’s workout equipment features the mascot, patterns, and colors from the brand.

Kettlebell

A kettlebell design features Athenic’s logotype, icon, and brand colors.


Sign

Outside the gym will feature a sign with the Athenic logomark. The logomark is a combination of the letter “A” in the logotype and an icon of a barbell.

Wall

A wall inside Athenic’s physical gym space uses words of encouragement, while prominently featuring the mascot and infographics.


Brand Guidelines

A 6 x 6-inch style guide provides rules on how to use Athenic’s branding elements.


Website

A 5-page website, found at AthenicGym.com, directs viewers to Athenic’s home page, and includes information about the gym, merchandise shopping, a roster of classes, subscription options, and contact information.

Photography

Photoshoot #2

Promotional Pieces

Handouts

A set of 4 x 6-inch postcards contain facts about the negative effects stemming from harassment in a gym environment to attract an audience of women, ages 16-35, to the Athenic website.

Subway Campaign

A set of posters, using a mix of bold type and photography, are posted throughout the walls of NYC’s subway line to advertise Athenic.


Infographics

Three 18 x 24-inch infographics combine photographs with typography to showcase positive research, the benefits of weightlifting for women’s health, and the negative effects stemming from harassment in a gym environment. These infographics will be posted throughout the gym’s space.


Social Media

Instagram

Twelve square Instagram posts promote Athenic’s gym and website, while sharing statistics about weightlifting.


Subscription Box

A box given to every new subscription member contains a t-shirt, stickers, tote bag, water bottle, thank you card, and wristbands.

Stickers

A set of 3 x 3-inch stickers feature branded design elements, including the Athenic logo, mascot, and slogans.

Tote Bag

A white tote bag with Athenic’s logo and mascot is sold on the website and is also included in the membership subscription box.

T-shirt

A t-shirt featuring Athenic’s logo, mascot, and slogan is sold on the website and is also included in the membership subscription box.

Wristbands

Silicone wristbands with brand slogans and words of encouragement are sold on the website and are also included in the membership subscription box.

Protein Shake Bottle

A plastic water bottle featuring the brand's logo, colors, and star pattern is sold on the website and is also included in the membership subscription box.

Thank You Card

A 5.5 x 4-inch thank you card is given to every new member who receives a subscription box.

Wrap

A weightlifting wrap features the brand’s colors and logo.
