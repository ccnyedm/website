Gallery of Student Work and Events

EDM Show

Students in Electronic Design and Multimedia classes participate in an annual Student Exhibition which presents work in areas of print, imaging, web, multimedia, video and 3D modeling and animation. This exhibition is usually scheduled in the Fall.


B.F.A.

BFA Thesis Exhibition

A requirement of the BFA program is the final Thesis Project, a one-semester advanced seminar in which students develop an individual, sustained creative project in a major area of concentration (such as print, internet, multimedia).

This investigation results in a Thesis Book documenting the students' work process as well as in the creative work itself. Projects range from animations, publication design, corporate identity design, digital video, 3D modeling and animation and various forms of imaging.

BFA Thesis projects and Thesis Book documentation are presented in a group exhibition at Graduation in late May in the Compton-Goethals Gallery.
