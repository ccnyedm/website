Jenny Chiang

Queens Cupping

Process Book

Abstract: My project is a branding identity for Queens Cupping coffee shop that sources beans from women-powered roasters and educates its customers about gender equality in the coffee industry; the brand concept will focus on the following qualities: women-oriented, expensive, upscale, artisanal, and sustainable. I will use full length and portrait photography of women in the coffee industry, flat and geometric illustration, minimalist design style, and recyclable packaging material to show the brand concept and what it stands for.

The coffee shop will target women in their early 20s to late 30s. I will design an identity system (logo/business card/letterhead/envelope), infographic poster, three Woman of the Month posters, a Tasting Wheel poster, menu, informative postcards, retail packaging (coffee bags, cups, stickers), website, Instagram page, a branding style guide, and environmental graphics mockup.

Check out Jenny's website at jencvisuals.com.

Project Elements

Logo Stickers

The logo stickers are a way to brand efficiently on eco-friendly cups & to add to giveaways and promotions for brand recognition. This contains soley the logo.

Business Cards

Two sided business card. One side is for business, the other side is a loyalty program. Seven stamps to a free coffee. For the stamp, Queens Cupping uses a lip stamp that embellishes the idea of the lip stain on the cup.

Menu

A table top menu placed on coffee tables & front cashier. It must always be unfolded, crisp and clean. This is a straightforward menu on the type of coffees we serve. The coffee origin includes the farm name & the flavor notes for coffee preference.

Stationary

Simple place to write down anything from coffee cupping information to typed up business deals.

Envelopes

Branded envelope to match identity system assets.

Postcards

Postcards create awareness about the coffee that Queens Cupping is promoting and selling.

Packaging

Coffee Sleeves

Coffee sleeves extend the brand. They incorporate a gold illustration that conveys richness.

Retail Bags

A branded retail bag wrap for the top of the bag. This includes every detail about the coffee and where its from. There will be a story included in the back speaking about the origins & what the coffee farm is known for.

Digital

Website

The Queens Cupping website features photographs and texts about women coffee producers along with menu and shopping information.
